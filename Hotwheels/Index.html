Tracks

Hot Wheels has 3 different categories about tracks.

  1. City
  2. Action
  3. Track Builder

The City tracks allow you to use your creativity to build a city to your liking. The Action tracks are interactive sets that range from $20 to $50. They can flip or fly off the track to another track. You can even race your friends and crash. The Track Builder are track pieces let kids build any unique stunts and races for an amazing experience. The possibilities are endless!

Cars

There are many different types of cars, such as:

  • Basics

  • DCcars

  • LargeVehicles

  • MarvelCars

  • StarWarsCars

  • Trucks

All hot wheels cost between $3 to $75. You can buy them in packs or just buy the best looking one online or in a store. The basics hot wheels are the vehicles made by hotwheels and their own creativity. The DC cars are Detective Comics themed. They could have superman or batman themed cars. Large Vehicles are vehicles that normally carry more than 1 car. Some are helicopters, boats, or maybe even a big truck! Marvel Cars are themed like the company Marvel. For example, you can buy a super reliable and cool spiderman hot wheel for just 20 bucks!

Accessories

Accessories are custom pieces for your cars or tracks. There are even little vehicles for children to ride in. Although the little cars for kids cost over $200, you can have endless amounts of fun. You want something a little cheaper, look at these amazing pictures of extra track pieces. There is even a huge loopty loop!
